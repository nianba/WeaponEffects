MeleeEffects(近战特效修改) is a toy mod that modifies all the melee weapons.
Just for cool slashing.After all I have not finished some specialization yet.


近战特效修改是一款修改所有近战武器的娱乐模组。
仅仅是有斩击效果而已。毕竟我还没有完成一些特化。


ALL sounds are from the game "DNF".
音效均来自游戏DNF。
